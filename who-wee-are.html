I started this company because being a working mom of two and pretty much the busiest person I know, I realized that other moms must deal with the same struggles that I do. Trying to gather up the kids and get everything in the car was always so frustrating and time consuming. Not to mention trying to get things on sale and at the best prices possible at the same time.



If you understand where I am coming from then we have a solution for you. Our goal is to provide parents hand picked outfits for their children from the most popular and top name brand companies out there and do it cost effectively. We guarantee that our outfits will be offered at 40-60 percent off of retail and every blessing package can be tailored for any budget. It will be like Christmas every month when your package arrives. Your child will absolutely love it. Let us be your child's personal clothing stylist and spend that much needed time with your family. Sometimes it's the little blessings in life that make all of the difference in the world.

Blessings,

Alicia Werle
