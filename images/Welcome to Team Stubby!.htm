Welcome to Team Stubby!

SGT. STUBBY:AN AMERICAN HERO is an upcoming computer-animated feature film based on the incredible true story of America's most decorated dog. After being rescued off the streets by a young Soldier on the eve of World War I, Stubby is given a home, a family and the chance to embark on the adventure that would define a century.

Featuring the voice talents of Logan Lerman ("Percy Jackson & the Olympians"), Academy Award nominee Helena Bonham Carter ("The Kings Speech," "Harry Potter" series), and Academy Award nominee Gerard Depardieu ("Cyrano de Bergerac," "Life of Pi").

Original score by award-winning composer Patrick Doyle ("Brave," "Thor," Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire")

Animation by Mikros Image ("The Little Prince," "Captain Underpants: The First Epic Movie")

The film is slated to premier on April 13, 2018.
